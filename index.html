About

I am currently a Software Engineer at ARRIS, an international telecommunications company in headquartered in Suwanee, GA. I work in the Research and Development CMTS verification department as part of the CLI team for the E6000 Converged Edge Router. I work mainly implementing features for the E6000 router, but I also am the team's expert on the CLI-TEST, a complex automated test that runs testing for thousands of commands. At this particular job, I use C++, Perl, and shell scripting for the majority of my work, but I have been exposed to Ruby and Go as well.

Before ARRIS, I attended Elon University. At Elon, I developed technical skills, intellectual curiosity, and passion for computing. As one of two Computer Science Fellows in my year, I participated in undergraduate research to explore the potential of using a tangible interface/volumetric display in 3D mobile application development. Inside and outside of the classroom, I have created several applications spanning topics from mapping to cloud computing to game design, further detailed in the PORTFOLIO section, linked above.
